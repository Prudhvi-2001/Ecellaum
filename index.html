Welcome to E-Cell AUM

The Entrepreneurship Cell was started by the students of Amity University Mumbai under the guidance of Amity Innovation Incubator – Noida to foster Entrepreneurship among the students on Campus. We at the Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) are focusing on encouraging students to start their enterprises which we believe is instrumental for our country to develop and aspire to cultivate fundamental skills required for real-world business, like Critical Thinking, Team Building and Group Dynamics, Networking with Partners, and understanding the legalities of starting their entrepreneurial journey Entrepreneurship-Cell (E-Cell) also conducts various inter-school workshops, seminars, and events to induce an entrepreneurial ecosystem and helps in providing a platform for individuals with creative minds and potential to become an entrepreneur in all spheres of life.

Think

The Sucessful Entrepreneurs are the ones who come up with new innovations and ideas. Throughout the various competetions and workshops,events, we are here to promote the culture of thinking to prepare students for fututre entrepreneurial challenges.

Build

Good startups are started by great people who have a knack at building projects ECell AUM organises many events to help people to come up with the best builders among aspiring entrepreneurs.

Inspire

The Key to stick through tough times and not giving up is to be relentlessly inspired to take on challenges. By organising various talks by sucessful entrepreneurs, ECell AUM aims to inspire students to take the path of Entrepreneurship.

It's not about ideas, it's about making them happen!

What we do?

We at the Entrepreneurship cell are focusing on developing the entrepreneurial spirit of young students which we believe is instrumental for our country to develop. Our vision is to carve out the niche for exploring the entrepreneurial mindsets. ECell captivates the spirits of youngester business visionaries in dispatching the endeavor as well as in carrying on with the existence with full energy.
